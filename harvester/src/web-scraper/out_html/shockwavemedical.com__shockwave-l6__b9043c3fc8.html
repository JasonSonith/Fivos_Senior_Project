Shockwave L6

Be large and in charge: Shockwave L6 is specifically designed for treating calcified peripheral artery disease (PAD) in large vessels above the knee.

Download Tech Sheet

Purpose-Built to Crack Calcium in Large Peripheral Vessels

Size up the situation with the Shockwave L6, our largest peripheral Intravascular Lithotripsy (IVL) catheter. Like our other IVL devices, Shockwave L6 uses sonic pressure waves to safely break up calcium in the artery wall and restore blood flow, specially designed for those larger femoral and iliac vessels. 

  • Large-vessel treatment: 8.0, 9.0, 10.0 and 12.0 mm diameter sizes 
  • Consistent high energy: Compact emitter design  
  • Focused treatment: 30 mm balloon length 
  • Support you need: 0.018” guidewire 
  • Ultra-low pressure: IVL therapy at 2-4 atm
